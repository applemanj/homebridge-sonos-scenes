Sonos Scenes

Discovery-driven Sonos workflow builder

Discover current rooms and groups, build switch-triggerable scenes, validate them against the live topology, and test them before saving.

Discover Refresh the live list of Sonos households, rooms, groups, favorites, and inputs.
Validate Checks your scene setup for errors against the current Sonos topology without changing anything on your speakers.
Run Test Actually runs the scene on your Sonos system now so you can confirm the grouping, source, and volume behavior.
Save To Homebridge Saves the current scenes into Homebridge so they persist and appear as HomeKit switch accessories.
